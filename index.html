How much does it cost to get a tattoo?

Tattoo costs vary widely based on size, complexity, and artist. Small tattoos can start at $50-$100, while larger designs can cost much more.

does it hurt to get a tattoo

Yes, it can hurt. Pain levels vary by location and individual tolerance, but most people find it manageable.

how to choose a tattoo artist

Research portfolios, read reviews, and ask for recommendations. Schedule a consultation to discuss your design and ensure you feel comfortable with the artist.

how to sign up for a session

Contact the artist or studio through their website, phone, or email. Provide details about your desired tattoo and book an appointment.

How Do I Care for My New Tattoo?

Follow our detailed aftercare instructions to ensure proper healing, prevent infection, and maintain vibrant results.
